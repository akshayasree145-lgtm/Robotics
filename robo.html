ROBOTICS

Robotics is the branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, operation, and application of robots.

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field that combines elements of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and artificial intelligence. It involves the development of robots that can perform tasks autonomously or with minimal human intervention.

Robots can be used in various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, agriculture, and space exploration. They can perform tasks such as assembly, welding, painting, and even surgery.
